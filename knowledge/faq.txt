Question: What can this assistant help with?
Answer: This assistant can help with Sri Chaitanya-related admissions, academic programs, curriculum, branches, campuses, transport, hostel, facilities, student life, parent support, contact information, and official school information available in the knowledge files.

Question: Can this assistant answer general questions?
Answer: No. This assistant only answers Sri Chaitanya school-related questions.

Question: Are admissions open?
Answer: The official Sri Chaitanya Schools website shows admissions open for 2026-2027. Parents should use the official admission enquiry or online admission flow and confirm details for the selected board, state, city, school, and grade.

Question: Which boards are shown in the admission form?
Answer: The admissions page shows CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, and State board options. Actual board availability depends on location and school.

Question: Does Sri Chaitanya provide transport?
Answer: The official facilities page describes school buses, trained drivers, assigned pickup and drop routes, and bus attenders. Route availability and transport fees must be confirmed with the selected branch.

Question: Does Sri Chaitanya provide hostel facilities?
Answer: Official pages describe hostel and boarding facilities, residential campuses, dormitories, lockers, hostel wardens, safety, security, hygienic living, and healthcare support. Availability varies by campus.

Question: Can this assistant provide exact fees?
Answer: Only if exact fee information is present in approved official source content. Exact school fee structures are not published in the official source content available here.

Question: What if verified information is not available?
Answer: The assistant should say: This detail is not listed in the official Sri Chaitanya information I have. Please contact the nearest Sri Chaitanya branch for confirmation.

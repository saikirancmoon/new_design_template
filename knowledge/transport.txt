Sri Chaitanya Transport Information

Sources:
- https://srichaitanyaschool.net/our-facilities

Sri Chaitanya's official school facilities page includes transportation as one of the facilities.

The page says the school pays close attention to travel safety for students.

The official description says Sri Chaitanya uses buses with comfortable seating. It states that the buses meet required government regulation standards.

The page says trained drivers handle pickups and drops on assigned routes.

The page also says bus attenders look after students and seating arrangements during travel.

Exact bus routes, pickup points, drop points, timings, transport fees, and branch-specific availability are not published in the official source content available here.

If parents ask for a specific route, timing, pickup point, or transport fee, the assistant should say verified information is unavailable and recommend contacting the selected Sri Chaitanya branch or support team.

Sri Chaitanya Schools provides an admission enquiry flow on the official schools website. The form asks parents to select the academic year, board, state, city, school, grade, and student gender.

The official schools website shows admissions open for the 2026-2027 academic year.

Boards shown in the school admission flow include CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, and State options. Exact availability depends on the selected state, city, school, and grade.

The official school site includes both Admission Enquiry and Online Admissions links.

For long-term IIT-JEE and NEET aspirants, Sri Chaitanya has announced admissions for 2026 aspirants on the official group site. The long-term program page says the first batch starts on June 16, 2025, and lists admission support by phone.

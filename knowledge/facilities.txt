The official Sri Chaitanya Schools facilities page lists transportation, library, sports, labs, hostel, digital labs, and fun club.

Transport:
The schools website says Sri Chaitanya pays close attention to student travel safety. It describes buses with comfortable seating, government regulation compliance, trained drivers, assigned pickup and drop routes, and bus attenders who look after students and seating arrangements.

Library:
The schools website says libraries include books suited to different reading levels, national dailies, journals, and audio or video collections that can support classroom learning. The group site also describes rich libraries with reference books, periodicals, magazines, and educational CDs for competitive exam preparation.

Sports:
The schools website says students are encouraged to balance work and play through sports programs. It mentions instructors, sports curriculum, mental strength, concentration, physical conditioning, coordination, planning, teamwork, inter-branch sports meets, athletics, team games, and indoor games.

Labs:
The facilities page says well-equipped labs support learning by doing through practical experiments and activities.

Digital learning:
Sri Chaitanya describes a technology-driven study approach. The student-life page says textbooks have been replaced with tabs for students from class five to seven in the school context, reducing study-material load and improving technology familiarity.

Health services:
The student-life page says Sri Chaitanya emphasizes a healthy mind and body, nutritious diet, in-house dispensary, round-the-clock medical attention, doctor visits, exercise, sports, meditation, and yoga. The food and health page describes nutritious meals, hygienic preparation, mineral water on residential campuses, and in-house healthcare support.

Social and cultural development:
Sri Chaitanya describes cultural meets, art competitions, music and dance competitions, festival celebrations, and opportunities for students to develop personality, confidence, heritage awareness, and community connection.

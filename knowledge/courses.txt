Pre-primary:
Sri Chaitanya's pre-primary curriculum focuses on foundational education. It includes language development, math skills, general awareness, knowledge and humanities, creative arts and crafts, and music. The school describes its Star Kids approach as warm, affectionate, curiosity-building, and focused on formative growth.

Primary:
Sri Chaitanya's primary education covers the first stage of formal schooling and emphasizes literacy, numeracy, environmental studies, moral values, social etiquette, communication, public speaking, teamwork, child-centric learning, and holistic growth.

High school:
Sri Chaitanya's high school curriculum emphasizes a career-oriented academic approach. The school describes a Techno Curriculum that integrates Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology for competitive-exam readiness while staying connected to the student's current class syllabus.

High school program categories mentioned by the official schools website include Techno, C-Batch, ICON, IPL, CO-IPL, Medicon, MPL, and S-Batch. These are described as preparation-oriented programs for IIT-JEE, NEET, and other higher-grade career pathways. Exact class eligibility and availability must be confirmed with the selected branch.

ACHARYA:
The ACHARYA program is described as a two-year academic pathway for students completing Class X under CBSE, IB, or ICSE and entering Class XI. It integrates board preparation with JEE or NEET foundation training. The program emphasizes structured learning, concept clarity, balanced preparation, focused academic environment, and continuous mentoring. Residential or day-scholar options may depend on campus.

IPL-IC:
The IPL-IC program is designed for JEE aspirants after Class 10 or SSC. It combines XII Boards with JEE Main and Advanced preparation and is aimed at students targeting strong ranks in JEE Main/Advanced or state engineering examinations. The page describes a structured curriculum, revision phases, regular tests, video solutions, app support, worksheets, and error or speed tests.

Olympiads:
Sri Chaitanya Olympiad content highlights conceptual learning, communication, public speaking, analytical skills, constant assessment, specially designed curriculum, skilled mentors, and integrated modules for CBSE, ICSE, and IB students. It references classes 3-11 for early Olympiad preparation.

Long-term JEE and NEET:
The official long-term program page describes preparation for IIT-JEE and NEET 2026 aspirants with expert faculty, personal attention, mentorship, doubt resolution, structured curriculum, rigorous testing, revision, full-length mock tests, motivational support, psychological support, hostel facilities, and focused academic environment.

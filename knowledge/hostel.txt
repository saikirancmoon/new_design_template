Sri Chaitanya official pages describe hostel and boarding facilities as part of student support in selected residential campuses.

The official school student-life page says some Sri Chaitanya schools in major towns and cities have sophisticated residential campuses. It mentions spacious dormitories, large dining halls, hostels with individual lockers, air-conditioned classrooms, full-time hostel wardens, and boarding-house care by wardens.

The official hostel and boarding page says Sri Chaitanya aims to provide students a comfortable, productive, and homely environment away from home. It describes disciplined and healthy hostel environments, hygienic living conditions, greenery and fresh air in campus surroundings, and attention to safety and security.

The hostel page says safety measures are adopted in hostel and boarding facilities, security personnel are deployed on campuses, and students are watched over through the day.

The long-term IIT-JEE and NEET program page mentions safe, disciplined hostel facilities and a focused academic environment for long-term aspirants.
